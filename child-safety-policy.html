Child Safety and Protection Against Exploitation Policy

Effective Date: 17/05/2025
Last Updated: 17/05/2025

1. Introduction

We are committed to maintaining a safe environment on our platform. This policy outlines our standards for preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and protecting minors from any form of harm.

2. Prohibited Content

We strictly prohibit any of the following:

  • Images, videos, or text depicting or suggesting CSAE
  • Grooming or predatory behavior targeting minors
  • Any content that sexually exploits minors

3. Age Screening and User Verification

We implement age screening mechanisms to restrict access to minors and help prevent misuse of the platform.

4. Content Moderation

Our team uses a combination of AI tools and human moderation to detect and remove harmful content, including potential CSAE-related content.
